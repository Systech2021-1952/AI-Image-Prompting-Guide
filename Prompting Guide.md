## **AI Image Prompting Guide** 

5 prompts to generate images that actually look like you 

## **01** 

## **Facial Feature Retention** 

The most important one. Tell AI to use your reference image and preserve every specific detail — eye shape, skin tone, nose, lips, jawline. The more specific you are, the more it looks like YOU and not some random AI face. 

## **PROMPT** 

Use the reference image provided. Retain all facial features exactly — eye shape, skin tone, nose structure, lip shape, and jawline. Do not alter or idealize any facial feature. The face should be photorealistic and identical to the reference. 

**02** 

## **Skin Texture** 

AI loves to make skin look perfect — like a plastic doll. Always specify realistic texture. This single instruction will immediately make your images look 10x more real. 

## **PROMPT** 

Realistic skin texture with natural pores, subtle imperfections, and no airbrushing or skin smoothing Avoid plastic or porcelain skin effect. The skin should look like a real photograph, not a digital rende 

## **03** 

## **Lighting Direction** 

Lighting completely changes the mood and how your face reads. Most people skip this and then wonder why the image feels off. Specify exactly where the light is coming from. 

## **PROMPT** 

Soft natural light coming from the left side, creating gentle shadows on the right. Warm tone, no harsh highlights. Lighting should feel like diffused daylight near a window — not studio flash. 

## **04** 

## **Expression Control** 

AI will guess your expression — and it almost always looks too intense or weird. Lock it in yourself. Be specific about the eyes too, not just the mouth. 

## **PROMPT** 

Neutral confident expression with a very slight natural smile. Eyes looking directly at the camera, relaxed — not forced or overly intense. The expression should look candid, like a natural moment was captured. 

## **05** 

## **Negative Prompting** 

Tell AI what NOT to do. This is underrated — one good negative prompt can cut bad outputs in half. Especially important if you want to avoid over-edited, unrealistic results. 

## **PROMPT** 

No cartoon or illustrated style. No heavy or dramatic makeup. No wide-angle lens distortion. No over-smoothed or airbrushed skin. No unrealistic body proportions. No fantasy or surreal elements.
